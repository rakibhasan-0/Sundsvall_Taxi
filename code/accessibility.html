Sundsvall Taxi's Accessibility Service

Sundsvall Taxi ensures top-notch service for everyone,

with extra care for accessible, easy services for people with

disabilities, guaranteeing comfort and convenience for all.

We have equipped our taxis with the latest technology and services so that people with disabilities can enjoy our service smoothly.

Including contactless payment or payment methods that are easier for people with mobility or dexterity issues

Allowance for service animals to accompany passengers with disabilities without additional charges

Vehicles equipped with ramps or lifts for wheelchair users, and enough space to accommodate the wheelchair inside the taxi

Our app makes it easier for riders with disabilities to get from A to B at the touch of a button.

Equipping taxis with basic medical supplies or features that can be useful for passengers with medical conditions, such as an emergency button
