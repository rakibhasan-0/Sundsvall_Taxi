Welcome to Sundsvall Taxi

We're here to make sure you get where you're going safely and comfortably.

Our cars are great, and our drivers know their stuff, so you can sit back and relax on your ride.

Airport Services

Heading to or from the airport? Let us make your journey easy. Our airport services are designed to provide you with a comfortable and timely ride, no matter if you're traveling solo or with a group. We track your flights to ensure we're always on time, and we offer spacious vehicles to accommodate all your luggage. Choose us for a worry-free start or end to your travels.

City Tour Services

Want to take a guided tour of Sundsvall City? Our city tour services are perfect for those who want to see the city from the comfort of their own home. We offer guided tours of the city, from the historic centre of Sundsvall to the stunning coastline. Choose us for an unforgettable experience in Sundsvall City.

Business Services

Sundsvall Taxi's exclusive services for business professionals include priority booking for swift service, access to luxury vehicles for comfort and style, and professional drivers offering discreet, top-notch service, complemented by convenient on-the-go amenities tailored to the needs of the business traveler.
